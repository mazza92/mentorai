### Phase 1: Planning & Research (15-20 min per batch)

**Step 1: Keyword Selection**
- Choose 5-10 related keywords from your priority list
- Group by intent: Informational, Comparison, How-to
- Verify search volume + difficulty in Google Keyword Planner

**Step 2: Template Assignment**
Match each keyword to content type:
- **How-To Guide** ‚Üí Step-by-step tutorials
- **Comparison** ‚Üí Tool/method comparisons
- **Listicle** ‚Üí "X Ways to..." articles
- **Educational** ‚Üí Deep-dive explanations

**Step 3: Research Collection**

- 2-3 concrete examples

**Use the templates below** with pre-filled structure.

### Phase 3: Quality Control (10-15 min per article)

- Add frontmatter metadata

- Cross-link with related articles
